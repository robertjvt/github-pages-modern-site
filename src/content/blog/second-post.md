Commercial Real Estate Development - Rotterdam

This commercial real estate project demonstrates our expertise in documenting large-scale developments. We provided comprehensive aerial imagery for marketing materials and progress documentation of a major Rotterdam development project.

Our drone services included:
- Progress documentation over time
- Marketing aerial photography
- Site context and scale imagery
- Architectural feature highlights
- Development showcase videos

The high-quality aerial content helped the developers attract investors and communicate the project's scope effectively to potential tenants and partners.
